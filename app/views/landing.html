Health insurance is confusing and CoveredU is here to help!

CoveredU is an educational website to help you understand the basics of how health insurance works. This site provides key terminology put in context through basic scenarios, information about the value of health insurance, and tips to get you started shopping for a plan.

Get started!

Please understand that the costs used in CoveredU are just educational examples. These costs are based on real health insurance plans, but actual costs may vary a lot based on your health insurance plan, age, where you live, and where you receive care.
